FAQs

How do I make a reservation at your hostel?
To make a reservation, simply visit our website and select your desired dates, room type, and number of guests. Follow the prompts to complete the booking process securely online.
Do you have parking facilities?
We may have limited parking available on-site or nearby. Please inquire about parking options when making your reservation.
Are pets allowed at the hostel?
Unfortunately, pets are not allowed at our hostel, with the exception of service animals.
Is the hostel accessible for guests with disabilities?
We strive to accommodate all guests, including those with disabilities. Please contact us in advance to discuss specific accessibility needs and ensure we can provide suitable accommodations.
What amenities are available in the rooms?
Our rooms are equipped with comfortable beds, individual reading lights, power outlets, and secure lockers for each guest. Some rooms may also include en-suite bathrooms or shared facilities, depending on the room type booked..
